2. What an AI agent actually is (no jargon)

An AI agent is a software program that answers your phone, talks to your customers like a real person, and books appointments directly into your calendar.

It's not a robot voice reading a script. It's a conversational AI that:

Your AI Agent Can:

  • Answer calls 24/7 — even at 2 AM on a Sunday
  • Understand what the customer needs (in plain English, Spanish, etc.)
  • Check your calendar for availability in real-time
  • Book the appointment and send a confirmation text
  • Answer questions about your services, pricing, and hours
  • Transfer to a human when the caller asks or the issue is complex
  • Follow up with leads who didn't book via SMS and email
  • Request Google/Yelp reviews after completed appointments

Think of it as your best receptionist — one that never takes a day off, never has a bad attitude, and handles 100 calls simultaneously without breaking a sweat.

3. The 3 automations every local business needs

Automation #1: The Phone Agent

This is the big one. An AI that answers every call your team can't get to.

HOW IT WORKS

Customer calls: "Hi, I need my AC fixed, it's not cooling."

AI Agent: "I'm sorry to hear that! I can get someone out to you today. What's your address?"

Customer: "123 Main St, Anytown."

AI Agent: "Perfect. I have a technician available between 2-4 PM today. Does that work?"

Customer: "Yes!"

AI Agent: "You're all set. I'll send you a text confirmation with the details. Is there anything else?"

Appointment booked. Confirmation sent. Zero human involvement.

Automation #2: The Follow-Up Agent

Someone fills out your website form or calls but doesn't book. The Follow-Up Agent reaches out automatically.

FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE

Minute 1: SMS: "Thanks for reaching out to [Business]! Do you still need help with [service]?"

Hour 24: SMS: "Hey, just checking in. We have availability this week if you'd like to book."

Day 3: Email: "Hi [Name], we helped [similar business] solve [their problem]. Here's what we did..."

Day 7: Final SMS: "Last check — are you still looking for help? Reply YES to book or NO and we'll stop reaching out."

This alone recovers 15-25% of leads that would have otherwise gone cold. At $300/job, even 5 recovered leads/month = $1,500/month back in your pocket.

Automation #3: The Review Agent

After every completed appointment, the AI automatically sends a review request. Happy customers get a direct link to Google Reviews. Negative experiences get flagged for you to handle personally.

Why this matters: businesses with 4.5+ stars get 3x more calls than those with 4.0 stars. A 0.1 star increase can mean $10K+ more revenue per year for a local business.
